Perceptual Bistability Modeling

Modeling Perceptual Bistability:
Impact of Neuronal Adaptation and Noise on Alternation Dynamics

This project is being conducted in the Yi lab at Dell Medical School at The University of Texas at Austin.

Project Description

In this project, we will study tumor evolution in the context of cancer metastasis by tracing the lineage of different samples/subclones within a single patient. Our methodology involves analyzing single nucleotide variant calls derived from datasets obtained through single-cell sequencing techniques like scRNA-seq or scATAC-seq.
